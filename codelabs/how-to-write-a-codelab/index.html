To indicate how long each slide will take to go through, set the Duration under each Heading 2 (i.e. ##) to an integer. The integers refer to minutes. If you set Duration: 4 the a particular slide will take 4 minutes to complete.

The total time will be automatically calculated for you and will be displayed on the codelab once you create it.
